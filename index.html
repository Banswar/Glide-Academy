About Glide Academy

Our story, mission, and the passionate team behind it all

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by former national champion Elena Chen, Glide Academy began with a simple mission: to make skating accessible, fun, and rewarding for everyone.

What started as small weekend classes has grown into one of the most respected skating schools in the region, with a dedicated facility and a team of expert coaches who share our passion for the sport.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of students discover the joy of skating, whether they wanted to learn for recreation or pursue competitive careers in figure skating and ice hockey.

Our Journey

The milestones that shaped Glide Academy

2010

Glide Academy founded by Elena Chen with weekend classes at a local rink

2013

Expanded to daily classes and hired our first team of professional coaches

2016

Opened our own dedicated skating facility with state-of-the-art ice rink

2019

First Glide Academy student competed in national championships

2022

Expanded facility to include pro shop and additional training areas
